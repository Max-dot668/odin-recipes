Chicken Quesadilla

Home

Description

These chicken quesadillas are filled with tender, seasoned chicken, gooey melted cheese, and crispy golden tortillas. They make a quick and delicious meal for lunch or dinner.

Serve them with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole for the perfect finishing touch. Simple, satisfying, and full of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then broil it until its cooked through.
  2. Cook the vegetables with the broiled chicken.
  3. Layer the mixture onto half of the tortilla, then top with cheese and bacon.
  4. Fold the tortillas and bake until the cheese is melted.
  5. Cut the quesadillas into wedges.
