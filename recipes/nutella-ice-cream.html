Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Large egg yolks
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa hazelnut spread (such as Nutella¬Æ)
  • Hazelnuts

Steps

  1. Combine milk, cream, and 1/2 cup sugar in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Beat egg yolks with the remaining 1/4 cup sugar in a bowl.
  3. Pour 1/2 cup warm milk mixture into egg mixture and stir.
  4. Place a strainer over a bowl and pour the warm custard mixture through the strainer.
  5. Chill mixture completely, about 1 hour.
  6. Transfer to a plastic container and place in the freezer for an hour before serving.
